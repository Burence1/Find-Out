About FindOut

The FindOut App is a multipage website consisiting of; the landing page, the App page and the Guide page. It is a project that manipulates birthdates to output suitable Akan names. The project has a guide page to aid and enhance clients’ experience with the app. Furthermore, this project is not limited to specific dates or centuries as it is capable of calculating anyone’s Akan name. Akan names are derived from Ghanaian naming culture, therefore, this app enables you to know your supposed Ghanaian name.
